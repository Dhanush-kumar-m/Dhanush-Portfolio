# ­ЪјЅ FestX 2026 - College Technical & Cultural Festival Landing Page

FestX 2026 is a premium, fully responsive, and modern college festival landing page built using HTML5, CSS3, and ES6+ Vanilla JavaScript.

## ­Ъје Theme & Dynamic Animation Features

*   **Default Dark Mode Aesthetics:**
    *   Initiates in a sleek **Dark Theme (Cyber Obsidian & Neon Violet)** by default to convey a tech-future college fest look.
*   **Soft Mouse Glow Follower:**
    *   A custom radial gradient glowing background follows desktop mouse coordinates smoothly behind text and cards (using LERP animation).
*   **Drifting Background Moving Pointers (Particles):**
    *   A set of 18 slowly moving, floating background indicators (`­ЪјЅ`, `­ЪЈє`, `­ЪјИ`, `­Ъј«`, `­Ъњ╗`, `­Ъјц`, `­ЪЋ║`, `Рюе`) matching the fest theme drift randomly across the screen, adding dynamic visual depth.
*   **Live Countdown Ticking Clock:**
    *   Ticking down live in days, hours, minutes, and seconds to the FestX starting date (October 15, 2026).
*   **Infinite Sponsors Logo Slider:**
    *   An infinite scrolling sponsors banner track that glides continuously and pauses on hover.
*   **Interactive Tabs Timeline Schedule:**
    *   Allows users to toggle timelines between Day 1 and Day 2 events smoothly.
*   **Dynamic Gallery Category Filters & Lightbox:**
    *   Sorts festival photos (Performances, Competitions, Workshops) on click and displays them in a high-resolution dark modal lightbox.
*   **Live Search Events Filter:**
    *   Filters 9 separate competition cards in real-time as users input search keywords.
*   **Contact Form & Newsletter Alerts Validation:**
    *   Validates names, emails, and 10-digit mobile phone numbers before triggering confirmation slips.
